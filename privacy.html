Storage

What Tactus stores, and where.

Everything you create, passages, bar structures, meters, accent maps, tempo shapes, is stored as files on your device. If you have iCloud Drive enabled, those files sync to your personal iCloud account so they appear on all your devices.

Apple operates iCloud and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. Tactus itself has no visibility into your iCloud files.

Location
Your device's local storage, and optionally iCloud.com.alanmolina.tactus in your iCloud Drive.
Format
JSON files with a .rhythmmap extension.
Who can see them
Only you, through your own Apple ID and iCloud account.
Developer access
Alan Molina and third parties have no access to your files.

Exported files

When you share a passage from the share sheet, a .rhythmmap file is created in your device's temporary storage and passed to whichever app or service you choose, AirDrop, Mail, Messages, Files. Tactus does not transmit this file anywhere on its own. You choose where it goes.

Local network

Ableton Link, in detail.

If you enable Link in settings, Tactus uses your device's local network to discover and connect to other Link-enabled apps on the same WiFi network, like Ableton Live, Logic Pro, or other iOS apps.

The Ableton Link SDK is developed by Ableton AG. When Link is active, it uses Bonjour (mDNS) for peer discovery. No personal data is transmitted.

Link, runtime behavior Local only
Link is a local-only protocol. The connection exists between devices on the same WiFi network. Nothing is sent to a server on the internet.
  • No data leaves your local network. No connection is made to any server on the internet.
  • Only tempo and transport state are exchanged. BPM and play/stop, with peers on the same WiFi.
  • Link is disabled by default. You opt in explicitly in settings.
  • Turning off the toggle ends all network activity immediately. No background reconnection.
Audio synthesis

Clicks are synthesized. Nothing is recorded.

Tactus generates click sounds using your device's audio hardware via AVAudioEngine. It does not record audio. It does not access your microphone. All audio is synthesized locally and plays through your speaker or headphones.

App Store & payments
